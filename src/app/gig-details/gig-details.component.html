How does this work?

    It's simple GigBus is a platform to enable folks from your town to see more gigs.
    By grouping together to arrange and drive down transport costs home from the gig.
    Sign up for a Gig by putting down a deposit.
    We then have 7 days to drive down the cost of transport by getting more people on the bus.
    If in 7 days we do not reach the target the deposit is refunded.
    Once the bus is full you pay rest of the cost transport via the GigBus app.
